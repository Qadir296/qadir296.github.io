I received my PhD from Sorbonne University in 2024. I obtained my Master’s degree in Computer Engineering from University Côte d’Azur, Nice, France, in 2021 and my Bachelor's in Telecommunication from Hazara University, Mansehra, Pakistan, with distinction in 2018. My research interests include the Internet of Things, Cybersecurity and privacy, and Knowledge base systems.
